# Headroom — Support

**Headroom** tracks your Claude, Codex, and Cursor usage limits with Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets, Live Activities, and Dynamic Island countdowns.

## Need help?

Email **headroomsupport@gmail.com** — questions, bug reports, and feature requests are all welcome, and you'll get a reply as soon as possible.

When reporting a problem, it helps to include:
- Your device model and iOS version
- Which account (Claude, Codex, or Cursor) is affected
- A short description of what happened

## Privacy

Headroom has no servers, no analytics, and no account of its own. Your credentials are stored only in your device's Keychain and are sent only to the provider they belong to. See the full [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).

## Frequently asked

**Is Headroom free?** Yes — every feature is included for free. Optional in-app tips are appreciated but never required.

**Where is my data stored?** Only on your device. Nothing is collected.

**How do I remove my data?** Settings → Manage Data → Remove all data & accounts, or simply delete the app.

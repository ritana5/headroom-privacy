# Headroom — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

## No data collection
Headroom has no servers, no analytics, and no accounts of its own. Nothing about you is sent to the developer or any third party.

## Where your data lives
The credentials you use to link an account are stored only in your device's Keychain, marked device-only so they are never synced or backed up elsewhere. They are sent only to the provider they belong to (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Cursor) to read your usage.

## What widgets see
Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets and the Live Activity read only usage percentages and reset times from a private app group — never your tokens or email.

## Donations
Tips are processed entirely by Apple through In-App Purchase. Headroom never sees your payment details, and tips are anonymous and optional. The app is fully functional for free.

## Your control
You can unlink any account or erase all stored data at any time from Settings. Deleting the app also removes everything it stored on your device.

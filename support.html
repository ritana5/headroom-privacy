Reporting a problem

To help resolve issues quickly, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Which account is affected (Claude, Codex, or Cursor)
  • A short description of what happened

FAQ

Is Headroom free? Yes — every feature is included for free. Optional in-app tips are appreciated but never required.

Where is my data stored? Only on your device. Headroom has no servers and collects nothing.

How do I remove my data? Settings → Manage Data → Remove all data & accounts, or simply delete the app.
